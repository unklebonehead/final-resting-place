About Unkle Bonehead

Unkle Bonehead, aka Ryan Ridenour, resides in central Michigan. Never really known as a "tech guy" but thinks he is. He's been known as Unkle Bonehead or just plain old Bonehead and Boner to his closest friends and family. He is most well known on the interwebs through his podcast and online radio shows.

He currently produces a live radio show/podcast  called Loud & Proud on MSPWaves and the  As the Wheels Roll podcast. He also is an indie musician and former long time running guitarist for the mid Michigan based band Slight Case of Death.

He has been an open source and privacy advocate for over a decade.

His former podcasts/radio shows were:

  • The Self Hosted Podcast
  • Ryan's Top Shelf
  • The Unkle Bonehead Show
  • D.J. for RFC radio
  • Loud & Proud Live - The Bush Radio & UBSN.net
  • NTROradio - Owner/dj
  • TruckinNutz Podcast
  • Break 19 Podcast

He is also a freelance podcast artwork creator. If you need any made please feel free to ask. He does it for free because it's fun to play with Gimp and Inkscape.
