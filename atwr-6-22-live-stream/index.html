How to add a Dbuzz button to a blog or website powered by Ghost

Time stamp 00:00 - 11:30

I've been building my new final resting place on the web by using Ghost as a headless CMS and github to build it and hosted on Netlify. It's similar to Wordpress only was less bulky and much more streamlined. Plus it uses Markdown or whatever you want. It really is much better than that crap Wordpress.

But there are two things I want in place before I take it live.

  1. I want an embedable player for my podcasts on Aureal
  2. I want a Dbuzz button on my posts so people can share them easily.

The 2nd one happened first and it was very easy to do.

This was extreemly easy. Just simply copy/paste and boom you're good to go! The hardest thing for me to do was to find and decide on a place to put it. Once I figured out how Ghost themes work it I decided on putting the button in my Authors byline on the post.hbs file. That way even if I have guest posters it will show next to theirs as well.

Plus it will be on every single post I make in the future and I dont have to add it each time.
