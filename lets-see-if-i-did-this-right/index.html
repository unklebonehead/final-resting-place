Lets See If I did This Right

Unkle Bonehead

Jun 24, 2021 • 3 min read

If I set this up right you are reading this on my new site Unkle Bonehead dot XYZ. I'll go more into why I chose that extension in a little bit but for now. Here is what you should know. This site should also (or soon will be) mirrored on IPFS. I will update you all on that when it will be released.

I'm home a lot more now

So that means that I will probably be way more active on the social media and podcasting. As well as this site. Where I can blog to my hearts content.  

Not that I will be throwing out posts every couple of hours. Believe me I am not that kind of ambitious at all. But I do want to put out a lot of content. And I like to do audio.

Aureal is very, very close to releasing their LIVE rooms. Along with that will come hosting and a fancy new embedable player. So I and many other podcasters can finally get rid of Anchor for hosting and holding or RSS feeds hostage.

I'm not  sure how these live rooms will work. You may have to join a server to listen somewhere. But I do know that they are will be able to restream to other platforms. ie VimmTv, Twitter, possibly even Discord servers. I do know that they are leveraging the rtmp protocol to do this. And it sounds like the part for hosting the files will be IPFS. Similar towhat 3Speak is doing with their desktop app and creating the Speak Network. Truly decentralized hosting for video files and creating it's own economy at the same time.

Why are you doing this? IPFS, Leaving Anchor, etc

Well I did this because I honestly dont see a very promising future. I'm not just talking about the internet. I'm talking world wide. There is no power held by any individuals anymore.

Governments are no longer about being responsible to the people's safety and welfare. They have shifted to serving the corporations and literally handed over control to the corporations. Who, while getting drunk on their quest for a monetary profit ran into an evil side effect. The ones running those corporations got a taste of power and control that they never should have gotten.

So now there thirst for both will soon lead us as a world into a future where only they hold the keys to what each of us will believe is truth. I beleve and can see that the new "flame of truth" is in Web 3.0. Through the use and development of technologies such as IPFS and blockchains such as Hive that flame's light will lead us out of the darkness of what Web 2.0 has enabled these mega corporations.

Why is Web 3.0 the new "Flame of Truth"?

Well for the simple fact is that it is uncensorable. Just by the way it works someone can never silence another's voice. Sure an individual dapp or front end could block your posts. But there is nothing that can stop you from going around it.

With Web 3.0 there is always a way around a problem. And one of the biggest is "Gatekeepers". The ones who control the access to info on the web. People like Google, ICANN, Amazon, etc. You know, the "infrastructure" companies. All they have to do is blacklist your domain, remove you from search results or even just shutdown your webserver.

With Web 3.0 and the IPFS technology your content is served from many different computers all over the world. So you can delete or shut down one but as long as there is one computer still online. Your content, your voice, is still out there.

That is true Freedom in the future.

Thats why I chose the TLD extension of .xyz. The last 3 letters of the alphabet. This is going to be my first step into my final resting place. I am digging in my heels and not holding back on what I say. They can do anything they want but I will be heard and I still want to hear from you. We all have a voice. We all want to be heard.

Web 3.0 is the future home of the Flame of Truth.
